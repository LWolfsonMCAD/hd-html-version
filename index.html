What is High Dramma?!

High Dramma is a team of actors/writers/comedians who have banded together in order to bring you the finest independent sketch comedy in the world (well, at least in Philadelphia).

So come on down and check us out! You can be sure to get a laugh...or two, because we take comedy so seriously, it's not even funny.
